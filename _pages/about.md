# About Me
* I am a Ph.D. student in the [School of Electrical Engineering and Robotics](https://www.qut.edu.au/about/our-university/organisational-structure/faculty-of-engineering/school-of-electrical-engineering-and-robotics) at [Queensland University of Technology](https://www.qut.edu.au/), advised by Prof. [Sridha Sridharan](https://staff.qut.edu.au/staff/s.sridharan) and Prof. [Clinton Fookes](https://staff.qut.edu.au/staff/c.fookes). My project is in collaboration with the [Robotics and Autonomous Systems Group CSIRO Data61](https://research.csiro.au/robotics/), advised by Dr. [Peyman Moghadam](https://people.csiro.au/m/p/peyman-moghadam).
* My research interests lie in the general area of robotic vision and scene understanding, particularly for the task of localization.
* I received my Bachelor’s Degree in Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering from the [University of Moratuwa](https://uom.lk/). My Bachelor's Thesis was adviced by Dr. Peshala Jayasekara.
